Hydroseeding

Hydroseeding essentially consists of the hydraulic application of a “blanket” of moisture retaining virgin wood fibers and tacifiers. Seed, fertilizer, lime and germination enhancers can also be added. It creates the perfect growing medium while protecting the soil from erosion and moisture loss. Germination and growth rates are increased substantially, and grow-in times and erosion are reduced substantially making it extraordinarily cost effective.
